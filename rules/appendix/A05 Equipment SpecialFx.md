# Equipment SpecialFx

Equipment SpecialFx are what differentiate a gun from a missile launcher or a laser. Some SpecialFx have an associated value, powerful SpecialFx have higher values, while debilitating SpecialFx have a negative value. Some SpecialFx cause no damage, but if they are combined with others that do they will.

## Auxiliary Equipment SpecialFx

### Positive Auxiliary SpecialFx

#### Searchlight

Mounted illumination Equipment for night operations. Turning it on and off takes 1 Take Time Action.

Illuminates the fire arc as daylight. Rating Grants 100m of usable illumination.

Cost= Rating

#### Illumination

The equipment releases an intense burst of light.

- Make an Enabled Attack.
- Success: Defender is Blinded by a number of turns equal to the MoS.
- Sensor Dependent SpecialFx is Immune to this Attack.

#### Concealed

The equipment is hidden until deployed.

- Cannot be detected while concealed, unless a physical search is done.
- Ignore one damage result if concealed.
- Deploying and retracting uses 1 Take Time Action.

$Cost=2$ Deploy Only

$Cost=4$ Deploy and Retract

#### Entangling

Chain, Nets, Bolas, whips, etc.

- Weapons Wielded Can Perform Ranged Grappling Attacks.
- Ranged Weapons Make Enabled Attack to Hit then a Grappling Attack with a Threshold defined by the rating.
- Ranged Grappling takes a number of Turns equal to the difference between the Rating and the Degree to remove.

$Cost=4$ For wielded weapons.
$Cost=Rating$ for ranged weapons.

#### Fire-Fighting

Dispenses Fire-fighting compounds that cancel 1 turn of Fire intensity equal to the Rating plus MoS of a Target. Constant use towards putting out a fire is an Effort Test with an Effort equal to the Fire Intensity minus The Rating. Use the MoS of the Attack unmodified.

$Cost=2*Rating$

#### Paint

The Equipment disperses a brightly colored paint or similar effect.

Provides no damage but marks a target until they remove the paint.

$Cost=1$

#### Shield

- Can bash using the shield xDR and the Shield Skill.
- If you use your shield as part of a collision you can add its armor value to your toughness for any damage you would receive from the collision.
- When not actively blocking, the shield adds one-half of its xDR (rounded down) to the vehicle’s base Armor on the flank side it is on.

$Cost=1*xDR$

#### Smoke

The Equipment dispenses thick smoke of whatever color desired.

- The smoke covers an area one-hex wide, horizontally and vertically.
- The smoke has an Obscurement value of 2.
- The smoke will dissipate after Rating in turns has passed.
- The smoke has no effect against armor, only visual and passive detection are affected by the Obscurement modifier. Active Sensors are not affected by smoke.

$Cost=1*Rating$

#### Target Designator

Target designators are used to lock-on and provide information to other Weapons.

To lock-on:

- The Spotter Takes the Aim Action target, the target remains designated as long as the Aiming continues.
- The Attacker, can Aim his attack up to the Aim bonus gained by the Spotter with their Aim Actions.
- The attack is resolved at the moment the guided equipment makes contact.
- Target Designation has no effect upon armor and does no damage by itself.

$Cost=4$

#### Winch

High-Strength Cable Winch. Max Size equals Rating.

- If the Host Machine is Larger than the Object, The equipment can Winch it towards the Machine
- If the Object is larger than the Host Machine, the Machine can hoist itself towards the Object.

$Cost=1*Rating$

#### Launcher

This equipment uses the throwing rules to deliver their payload.

$Cost=2$

#### Remote

The Equipment can be activated by remote-command.

- This requires either a line-of-sight to its location, a satellite uplink or a forward observer in contact.
- Activating the Equipment requires An Electromagnetic Engineering Skill Test.
- Remote Equipment is affected by Electronic warfare.

$Cost=1$

### Negative Auxiliary Equipment SpecialFx

#### Fuse

Grenades and Explosives use the Fuse SpecialFx.

Fuse means the Equipment will activate after the number of turns has passed. The Fuse last for 3 seconds per Rating value.

- If an Enabled Attack is made to throw the Equipment 3 turns before activation, it can be considered to activate on impact.

$Cost=-1*Rating$

## Ranged Weapon SpecialFx

### Positive Ranged Weapon SpecialFx

#### Bipod / Tripod

- The equipment is considered braced while prone.
- Negates the unwieldy SpecialFx when braced.
- Opening and closing the bipod requires 1 Take Time Action.
- The wielder can't move or use Adjustments while wielding the braced weapon, and must let go for free in order to do so.

#### Laser Sights

Low powered laser pointer. Some are plainly visible while others use infrared or ultraviolet vision.

Can laser Targets up to 100mt per Rating, Grants 1 free starting Dice on the Aim Action. Visible Lasers can alert the target with a Threshold 5 Sense Attribute Test modified by facing.

$Cost=1*Rating$

$Cost=2*Rating$ if infrared or ultraviolet individually

#### Scopes

Scopes are made with a distance in mind.

- Aim Actions from the chosen range start with +1D for free.
- Aim Actions below the chosen range start with -1D for free.
- Aim Actions above the chosen range are unaffected.

- Picking this SpecialFx multiple times turns the scope into a variable scope.

$Cost=4$

#### Silencer

Sound muffler for weapons.

- Sound is muffled after a common barrier like house walls.
- Listeners apply the Attacker's Facing penalties to their Sense Test. For Example: someone behind the Attacker would make a Sense Test with a -2 Penalty.

$Cost=2$

## Melee Weapons SpecialFx

### Positive Melee Weapon SpecialFx

#### Fencing Weapon

The weapon Gains +1D when deflecting and +1D if you do a spotdodge as part of your deflection.

Fencing weapons can’t parry flails.

$Cost = 2$

#### Flails

Targets attacked by flails receive a -1D penalty to their block or deflection, a shield receives no penalty to their block or deflection, and fencing weapons can’t parry flails.

$Cost = 3$

## Weapon Qualities SpecialFx

### Positive Weapon Qualities

#### Adhesive

Adhesive systems stick to the target before acting. Optionally it can be purchased to detach with a Take Time Action. The Attack Stays attached for a number of turns equal to the Rating.

To detach an adhesive Equipment by the defender, an Enabled Defense test is made versus the attacker's 4+MOS of his attack

$Cost= 2*Rating$
$Cost= 4$ for the detaching ability.

#### Target Size X

The Equipment is specially designed to affect different sized targets. Targeting rules uses the equipment size instead of the wielder size. Rating = the difference of the wielder size and the target size (always positive)

$Cost= 1*Rating$

#### Aerosol

The Equipment discharges small particles.

- Any energy weapon or target designator going through the mist it is considered to have traveled an extra range increment.
- The mist dissipates at the end of the next turn.
- Aerosol has no effect against Armor.

$Cost= 2$

#### Antimissile

The Equipment can do the Antimissile even if it is not an Automatic weapon. Their Rating represents the Available RoF to use by the weapon.

$Cost= 1*Rating$

#### Anti-Structure

The Equipment has been designed to damage inert structures, the damage against structures is doubled.

$Cost= 3$

#### Area Effect

The Equipment affects an area. This turns the Base Rating into the Area of Effect.

$Cost= 5+Rating$

#### Explosive

Explosives attacks don't require rolls, They also Must have an Area Effect SpecialFx. The Explosion Threshold is defined by the area in which the defender is located when the explosive goes off. Each defender must roll their defense accordingly.

| Distance | Threshold |
| -------- | --------- |
| Short    | 5         |
| Medium   | 4         |
| Long     | 3         |
| Far      | 2         |

If an Explosive with the Fuse SpecialFx is thrown at the Target to activate on Impact, a successful attack adds the MoS to the explosive's defense roll threshold for the target only.

$Cost = 4$

#### Armor-Piercing

The Equipment is highly efficient penetrating armor.

- Successful Attacks Reduce the Armor Value of the target by the rating value of the SpecialFx. This reduction applies only to the attack. Armor damage occurs as normal. Armor cannot be reduced below 0.

$Cost=1.5*Rating$

#### Energy-Tracking

The Equipment Gains +1D to Attacks against Targets that have used Sensors or Communication.

$Cost=1$

#### Guided

Guided systems correct during flight, and can use targeting information supplied by a forward observer.

- Guided equipment make an attack roll using the skill of the person supplying the targeting information.
- If the attack takes more than one turn to reach its target the unit providing the targeting information keep Aim of the target until it reaches.

$Cost=2$

#### Elemental

Elemental equipment ignite, cool, or electrocute their targets. They do not do damage normally, they cause an elemental effect of Intensity equal to (MoS + xDR). The effect only last 1 second unless mixed with the persistent SpecialFx. Continuous firing is counted towards the amount of seconds exposed to the elemental source.

$Cost=2$

#### Indirect Fire

The Equipment can work indirectly, it does not need a direct line of sight to its target as long as it has a forward observer.

$Cost=2$

#### Liquid

The Equipment dispenses as a liquid.

- Double the target’s Armor.
- If the Attack is Blocked or Deflected it is still considered to have touched the target.
- Only evasion can avoid contact.

$Cost=3$

#### Persistent

Persistent systems cause the target to suffer the attack’s effects for a number of turns equal to the Degree of Success + Rating. Exposure is counted backwards from the highest value first to the lowest value last.

$Cost=2*Rating$

#### Seeking

The Equipment can Attack a moving target more than once If the attack fails.

- The Attacker Makes an Enabled Attack once. This becomes the Seeking Threshold for subsequent attacks.
- The Defender defends against the Seeking thresholds normally.
- The Defender must defend a number of turns equal to the Rating.

Missile Attacks can be reduced by antimissile fire. Adjust the Seeking Threshold accordingly.

$Cost= 5*Rating$

#### Smart

The equipment can operate by itself.

- Their Skill Level is Equal to their Rating. it cannot go above 3.
- It gains an exclusive action for itself.

$Cost= 8+ 2*Rating$

#### Wide Angle

The system covers a wide area, affecting multiple targets at once. The attack works using the suppressive fire rules. But covers an entire hex facing.

$Cost= 3$; 10 degrees
$Cost= 6$; 60 degrees
$Cost= 15$; 180 degrees
$Cost= 30$; 360 degrees

### Negative Weapon Qualities

#### Location-Specific

The Equipment can only target certain locations.

When a Failure is Gained the Subsystem Location is not rolled randomly but specified by this SpecialFx. You cannot pick the Crew Subsystem for this SpecialFx

$Cost=-3$

#### Attenuating Damage

The Equipment loses a portion of its damage potential over distance.

- The Rating is subtracted from the Damage Multiplier for each range band beyond Short.

$Cost= -5*Rating$

#### Fitness Requirement

Crossbows and bows have a draw weight.

- Ranged Weapons take a number of Take Time Actions equal to The difference between the Rating and Fitness to be ready to shoot.
- Melee weapons if the fitness is less than the rating you gain a penalty to attack rolls equal to the difference.
- Characters cannot wield the weapon if the Difference is higher than 3.

$Cost= Rating$ Max 6.

#### HEAT Seeking

These systems rely on energy transmission rather than impact to damage their target. They thus take less space and are lighter, but they can be counteracted by special armor and defense systems.

$Cost= -3$

#### Cumbersome

The Equipment, slows the wilder Top Speed by 1 range per Rating.

$ Cost = 4\*Rating$ Max 4.

#### Minimum Range

The equipment is unable to affect a target at a distance defined by the Rating.

$Cost= -2*Rating$

#### Nonlethal

Nonlethal knocks out characters without killing them, The attack uses the xDR of the weapon as a fatigue effect, This fatigue is not added to the character's fatigue, but the MoS of the attack is.

$Cost= -10$

#### Unbalanced

Unbalanced weapons must be 'set' after each attack.

$Cost= -5$
