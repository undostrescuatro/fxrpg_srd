## Adding a Cinematic Feeling

### Cinematic Offence

- Ignore self inflicted damage from unarmed strikes to armor.

### Cinematic Defense

For a more action-oriented feel, apply one or more of the following options for your players and important NPCs only:

- A Skill can Deflect or Block 1+CPX Attacks per turn.
- You can Deflect slow projectiles.
- You can Block Fast projectiles.
- You can Deflect Fast projectiles!
- Ignore penalties for evading multiple attackers.

### Cinematic Resilience

- Ignore Trauma Tests when receiving wounds.
